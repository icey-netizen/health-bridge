Cardiology

Comprehensive heart and cardiovascular disease management

Our cardiology department offers advanced diagnostic testing, interventional procedures, and comprehensive treatment plans for all cardiovascular conditions. Our board-certified cardiologists provide expert care for heart disease, arrhythmias, hypertension, and other cardiac disorders with the latest technology and treatment options.

Neurology

Expert care for nervous system disorders

Our neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions including stroke, Parkinson's disease, epilepsy, migraine, and dementia. We utilize advanced neuroimaging and diagnostic techniques combined with evidence-based treatment approaches for optimal patient outcomes.

Hepatology

Specialized care for liver and digestive conditions

Our hepatology department provides comprehensive care for liver diseases, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and digestive disorders. We offer advanced liver function testing, therapeutic interventions, and nutritional support for optimal liver health and function.

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents

Our pediatrics department provides comprehensive healthcare for children from newborn through adolescence. We offer preventive care, immunizations, acute care, and chronic disease management with a child-friendly approach and family-centered care philosophy.

Eye Care

Comprehensive ophthalmology and vision care services

Our eye care department offers complete vision assessment, treatment of eye diseases, surgical interventions, and prescription services. From routine eye exams to advanced procedures like cataract and LASIK surgery, we ensure optimal vision and eye health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, procedures, and healthcare offerings

How do I schedule an appointment at HealthBridge?

You can schedule an appointment through our online appointment system, by calling our main office at +1 (555) 123-4567, or by visiting us in person. We make every effort to accommodate your schedule within 48 hours.

What insurance plans do you accept?

HealthBridge accepts most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross, and many others. Please contact our billing department at +1 (555) 123-4567 to verify your specific coverage.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, government-issued ID, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from other providers. Also arrive 15 minutes early to complete check-in paperwork.

Are your medical records available online?

Yes, HealthBridge provides secure online access to your medical records, test results, and appointment summaries through our patient portal. You can also request printed copies of your records at any time.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, HealthBridge operates a fully staffed emergency department 24/7 with trauma specialists, advanced imaging, and life-saving equipment. For medical emergencies, call 911 or go to our emergency room immediately.
